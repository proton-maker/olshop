About Escape from Tarkov


Escape from Tarkov is a multiplayer first-person shooter video game. It is a hardcore open world FPS featuring RPG elements in the fictional war-torn region of Tarkov. You can join one of two ideologically opposed Private Military Corporations: corporate backed USEC or Russian-created BEAR.

Escape from Tarkov allows you to complete story-driven quests either alone or with comrades, raiding huge locations to unravel the mysteries driving the war.

Level up and train nearly 100 skills in RPG fashion to enhance your character's prowess and survive the harsh gameplay. Form a clan with other players and hunt down vagabonds or create a trade network to reap enormous profit.


FAQs


Q: What are the different editions of Escape from Tarkov?
A: The game offers multiple editions: Standard, Left Behind, Prepare for Escape, and Edge of Darkness. Higher editions provide more starting gear, larger stash size, and other bonuses.

Q: What are the system requirements for Escape from Tarkov?
A:
  • Minimum: Intel Core i3, 8GB RAM, NVIDIA GeForce GTX 950.
  • Recommended: Intel Core i5, 16GB RAM, NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or higher. A stable internet connection and at least 12GB of storage are also required.

Q: What are Scavs in Escape from Tarkov?
A: Scavs are AI-controlled enemies (Scavengers) that roam the map looking for loot. Players can also play as Scavs to loot and escape without risking their main character's gear.

Q: How do I improve my stash size in Escape from Tarkov?
A: Upgrading to higher account editions, like Edge of Darkness, provides a larger stash. You can also increase stash size through in-game hideout upgrades.
